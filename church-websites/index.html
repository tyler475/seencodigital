// questions

Asked often. Answered honestly.

Will our site look like everyone else's?

No. The template is the framework underneath. Your colors, logo, photography, and words are what people actually see, and no two churches share those.

Can we add more pages later?

Yes. Additional pages can be added anytime as a small one-time cost, or we can talk about a custom build if your church outgrows the template.

Who updates the site after launch?

You can, from your phone. Every care plan includes a login to the seen.co portal, where you edit content beside a live preview of your site. And if you'd rather just send us the change, a real person handles it.

What if we don't have a logo or good photos?

That's exactly what the Build + Rebrand package is for. We refresh your logo and give you a social graphics package so everything matches from day one.

Do we own the site?

Yes. It's your domain and your content. Monthly Care covers hosting and upkeep, but the site belongs to your church.

How fast is "live in days"?

Once we have your content and assets, most builds launch within a week. The slowest step is usually gathering photos, and we'll help with that too.
